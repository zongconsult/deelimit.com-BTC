A Lil Bit Serious

2014 Mixtape

The Mixtape – A Lil Bit Serious is a dedication of hard work. A Lil Bit Serious in whatever you do and surely you shall embrace success. Coming off the streets of Bolgatanga where music receives no attention, I thrived to put this together. I want to thank the Lord Almighty for his blessings of Good health and the gift to entertain. My Prayer is that my music changes lives, also I want to thank my Mama for her immense prayers and support. God bless you mama Big shout outs to Okomfour Kwadee for such inspiration, to @Xtremegh of http://www.hypegh.com and Salase of @FireblazeEnt for their support and efforts to put this out there. To my fans (TheDeeLimitFamily) who buy, download and share my music I say you are the reason that keeps me going and I’m grateful for your support. A Lil Bit Serious represents the struggle to put BeeTown in the Ghana music scene. The E.P #BeeTowNconneCt drops September 30th 2015 and features Artists Like King Ayisoba, Okomfour Kwadee & SooreBia just to mention a few.
