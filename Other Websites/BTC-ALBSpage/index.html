Biography.

BeeTown Finest, King of the North Name is Dee Limit.
Upper East (Bolgatanga) based artist who blends Frafra, Twi & English in his music.
As an artist with such a unique style, he is the first of his kind in the Ghanaian music scenes.
The Artist/Songwriter at an early age, discovered his writing and rapping skills. "I had my 1st studio recording in 2005", and after his University education in 2013 which he studied Computer Science, Dee Limit is to channel his energy into growing the music industry in Upper East.
The artist is in studios recording his 2015 BeeTownConnect E.P
